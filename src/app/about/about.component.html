About Us

INTRODUCTORY :

Dronacharya Bahuuddeshiya Sanstha was established in 2010. This institution is located nearly 75kms towards North in Amravati district in the twin small adjoining cities i.e. (Paratwada and Achalpur) block. There are in total 11 Colleges and Schools in this area where the strength of the students is quite big. For enhancing their individual skills especially in the area of sports they don't have much opportunities and facilities and proper coaching available in this region. ‘Dronacharya Bahuuddeshiya Sanstha’ is taking an initiative to arrange inter College and inter block competitions for these children. These students were not having much more scope to compete on District, National as well as international levels.

Dronacharya Bahuuddeshiya Sanstha is having the dream not only to reach to this local twin cities but also to reach in the interiors of tribal region nearby and enhance the skills of the tribal students specially in the stream of "Archery".Dronacharya Bahuuddeshiya Sanstha is trying to facilitate those students who are the seekers and having the dreams to fly high in the field of Archery.
