Recurve Archery

  • Features limbs that curve away from the archer.
  • Simple design, used in the Olympics.
  • Often a takedown design for portability.

Compound Archery

  • Uses cams and pulleys for mechanical advantage.
  • Equipped with stabilizers and advanced sights.
  • Popular for precision target shooting and hunting.

Indian Bow

  • Featured in Hindu mythology, symbolizing strength and divinity.
  • Lightweight, flexible bows used in rural areas.
  • Single-piece wood bows used by ancient Indian archers.
